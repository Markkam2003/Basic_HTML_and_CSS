Accommodations

About Academy Cinemas

Located in the beautiful city of Portland, Oregon's Pearl district!

Our reels have been spinning since 1959. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles is our pleasure!

Academy Cinemas is family owned and operated, offering a comfortable theme and relaxing setting. Even though we are small, we offer BIG movies!

We are located on the corner of West Avenue and Hartford Lane, come see us anyday!
